Seattle Shower-Door Installation & Glass Experts

From Ballard bungalows to Bellevue high-rises, igogreenenterprise is the name Seattle homeowners trust for precise, eco-conscious shower-door installation. Our installers have spent more than a decade crafting frameless, semi-frameless, and framed shower enclosures that stand up to the moist Pacific Northwest climate while adding instant value to the property.

We partner with trusted local glass suppliers to source custom-cut panels to your exact measurements. Our experienced installers handle every step with care — ensuring precise fitment, clean silicone lines with no smudges, and a watertight seal that stands up to daily use. Whether you’re replacing a tired builder-grade slider or designing an ultra-modern frameless shower door with Starphire™ glass, we handle the entire process in-house: laser measurement, custom hardware selection, and professional mounting.

Service area: Seattle, Kirkland, Redmond, Issaquah, Tacoma, Kent, Auburn, Federal Way, and the wider King/Pierce/Snohomish County region. We also partner with local remodelers and commercial-build GC’s who need safety glass, partitions, or glass railings on a tight timeline.

Why choose a local specialist?

  • On-site consultations anywhere in Western Washington—often within 1-3 Days.
  • Premium hinges & hardware in stainless, matte-black, brass, and brushed nickel.
  • Eco-friendly disposal of old tempered glass and aluminium frames.
  • Up-front, itemised quotes; no hidden trip fees.
